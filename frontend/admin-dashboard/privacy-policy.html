Privacy Policy & Child Protection

Data Protection

✅ All user data is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption

✅ Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt rounds

✅ JWT tokens expire after 7 days for security

✅ HTTPS enforced for all data transmission

Child Online Protection

✅ Students under 18 require parental consent

✅ All teacher-student communications are monitored

✅ No personal information shared publicly

✅ Content filtered for age-appropriateness

✅ Reporting system for inappropriate content

Data Collection

We collect: Name, Email, Grade Level, Learning Progress

We DO NOT collect: Physical address, Phone numbers, Financial information

Your Rights

✅ Access your data anytime

✅ Request data deletion

✅ Export your learning history

✅ Opt-out of notifications
